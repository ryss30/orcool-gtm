Questions

The honest answers.

How does multi-brand work?

One Orcool account covers every client you run. You add each retainer as a separate brand with its own competitors, category, and Slack digest. They don't bleed into each other. When you close a new account, add the brand and the first weekly report runs that night.

How does sign-in work?

Paste the MCP URL into your client. It opens a browser tab, you enter your email, and we send a one-click magic link. No password, no separate account. After that the tools are live in every conversation, across all your client brands.

What exactly are Alerts?

Alerts are press and media moves: a competitor launching in a new market, making a strategic acquisition, running a major sponsorship or event. They are not ad-creative reports. They come from monitoring the press, not the ad library.

Is the data real, or model guesswork?

Real. Live competitor creative across 40+ markets, structured and scored. The why on a pattern is grounded in what's actually running and holding in spend, not what a model thinks sounds plausible. Try it below — pick a category and market.

What does it cost?

Connecting is free, first report is free. All three feeds run about $250/mo per brand, on a card, cancel anytime. The full loop with finished creative starts at $3K/mo plus a media share. We don't sell concepts on their own.
