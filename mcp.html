Orcool MCP Server

Connect Claude to Orcool Studio, an AI ad-creative research and production platform that takes market signals to scored creative concepts to finished video, in one pipeline.

What you can do with it

  • Research competitors and audiences: pull competitor references, inspiring creatives, top-performing ads, brand trends, and global signals directly in Claude.
  • Generate and score creative concepts: ask Claude to produce ad concepts for any brand, format, and duration. Each concept is AI-scored for potential.
  • Produce video creatives: generate creative packs, individual videos, footage, and AI avatars without leaving your conversation.
  • Analyze reviews and build target audiences: summarize app-store or product reviews, extract themes, and define structured target audiences.
  • Organize and govern creative work: manage folders, policies, and brand settings across your Orcool workspace.

Requirements

  • An Orcool Studio account. Start a free 7-day trial at app.orcool.com. No credit card required.
  • Available globally, with no regional restrictions.

How to connect in Claude

  1. Open Claude and go to Settings > Connectors (the custom connector panel).
  2. Add a new connector with the URL: https://mcp.orcool.com
  3. Claude initiates an OAuth 2.0 sign-in flow. You receive a magic link by email, no password needed.
  4. Click the magic link to authorize, and the connector becomes active.

Transport: Streamable HTTP (recommended) and SSE are both supported. Dynamic Client Registration (DCR) handles client setup automatically, so there is no client secret or manual app registration required.

Authentication

  • Protocol: OAuth 2.0 with Dynamic Client Registration (DCR).
  • Sign-in method: passwordless magic link sent to your Orcool account email.
  • No secrets to manage: DCR means the MCP client registers itself automatically. You do not share any client ID or secret manually.
  • Sessions persist across conversations until you revoke access from your Orcool account settings.

Troubleshooting

Cannot connect or connector not loading. Verify the URL is exactly https://mcp.orcool.com. Remove and re-add the connector if the OAuth flow did not complete.

Magic link not received. Check your spam folder. The link expires in 10 minutes. Retry the sign-in flow to request a new one, and make sure the email matches your Orcool account.

"Brand not found" or missing brandId. Run studio_list_brands first to retrieve the correct brandId. Brand names are not accepted directly by most tools.

Generation is taking a long time. Video, avatar, and creative pack generation can take 30 seconds to several minutes. Use get_job with the returned jobId and poll until the status is completed or failed. Do not resubmit the request.

Trial expired. Your 7-day trial has ended. Visit app.orcool.com to upgrade or contact support.

Tools not visible after connecting. Disconnect and reconnect the connector to trigger a fresh capability discovery. If the issue persists, contact support.
